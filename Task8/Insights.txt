Sales Dashboard Insights — Superstore Dataset

1.Overall Performance:
The total sales reached ₹887.87K with a total profit of ₹108.78K, reflecting a strong sales volume and steady profitability across regions.

2.Regional Sales Trends:
The West region outperformed all others, generating the highest revenue, followed by the East region. The Central and South regions contributed comparatively lower sales, highlighting potential areas for sales improvement.

3.Category-Wise Insights:
The Technology category dominates overall sales with 38.14% share, followed by Furniture (32.63%) and Office Supplies (29.23%), showing higher consumer demand for technology-related products.

4.Monthly Sales Behavior:
Monthly sales exhibit a slight downward trend after the initial months of the year, suggesting a need for stronger marketing and promotional strategies to boost mid-year performance.

5.Profit Margin & KPIs:
The dashboard reflects a Profit Margin of ₹48.63K and an average sales value of ₹219.82, indicating a solid base for consistent growth. However, deeper profit optimization is required in lower-performing regions.
